The Husk Miner is found within the Crystal Peak. It possesses an effective short and long range attack, and can prove especially tricky to deal with when in groups.

Behavior

  • At longer ranges, the Husk Miner will throw a claw-pick at the player in a arching path aimed at the players position.
  • If the player is too close to the Husk Miner, it will instead swipe at the player with its claw-pick.
  • After completing its attack, the Husk Miner will take a few paces, either towards or away from the player.

Tactics

  • The thrown claw-picks can be batted away with the nail. If hit back at the Husk Miner, it will deal 5 damage to it.
  • A better strategy is to avoid the thrown claw-pick and close the distance to deal a hit before backing out of range of its short range swipe. After the swipe, it is safe to press back in for another hit, until the Husk Miner is dead.

Locations

Husk Miners can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
