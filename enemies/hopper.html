The Hopper is a ground based enemy found throughout Kingdom's Edge. They appear in great numbers and will respawn if the player exists and reenter their room.

Behavior

  • Hoppers will jump along at a set rate, turning around when they bump into a wall. They do not react to the player in any way.

Tactics

  • They can be kept at bay by knocking them back while slowly retreating.
  • Hoppers appear in large numbers, are difficult to jump over, and can be overwhelming if you are backed into a corner. Try to have an escape route in case more than you are ready for appear.

Locations

Hoppers can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
