The Fungoon is a relatively docile floating enemy found within the Fungal Wastes.

Behavior

  • Fungoons will very slowly float in the direction of the player.
  • If the player is below them, a Fungoon will shoot out of stream of deadly gas at them, which lingers for a while.

Tactics

  • Stay above them, or at the same height to completely avoid their deadly gas.
  • Their large size, slow movement, and lack of upward attack makes them perfect for nail jumping.

Locations

Fungoons can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
