Mantis Warriors are fierce ground based enemies found in the lower reaches of the Fungal Wastes, near the Mantis Village.

Behavior

  • The Mantis Warrior will bow, then dash forwards while slashing in front of itself.
  • If its dash takes it too close to the player, it may back up before attacking again.
  • Once the Mantis Lords are defeated, they will not attack unless hit first.

Tactics

  • If you have enough room, jumping over their attacks to hit them from above can be effective, as they have no upwards attack.
  • When in an enclosed area, walk towards them immediately after their attack finishes, land one hit, and turn and dash away to safety.

Locations

Mantis Warriors can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
