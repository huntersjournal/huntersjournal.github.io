The Leaping Husk is a ground enemy which, as its name suggests, can jump through the air to attack the player. It is found predominately in the Forgotten Crossroads. Most of them are replaced by Slobbering Husks after the Forgotten Crossroads become infected.

Behavior

  • The Leaping Husk will wander back and forth until it catches sight of the player.
  • Leaping Husks will quickly crouch before making an arching leap towards the player's position. After landing, it will pause then repeat its jump if the player is still in sight.

Tactics

  • Leaping Husks can be most safely dealt with be remaining just out of range of their leap, and hitting them when they land.
  • They can also be intercepted and batted back in midair, causing them to land short of their target.
  • It is also possible to stand close to them befor their leap, and have them jump directly over the players head, missing them entirely.

Locations

Leaping Husks can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
