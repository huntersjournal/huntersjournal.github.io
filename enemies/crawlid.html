  • Timid scavenger that crawls through the caverns looking for food.
  • Dull and pathetic. Hardly worth killing.

Health: 8

The Crawlid is a simple ground based enemy found in many of the starting areas, including the Howling Cliffs and the Forgotten Crossroads.

Behavior

  • Crawlids will walk along the ground at a constant speed, turning around when they encounter a ledge or wall.
  • Unlike the TikTik, they do experience knockback, but will not stop when hit.

Tactics

  • Because of their simple movement, Crawlids are good targets for nail-jumping, though simple attacks work just as well.
  • Their low health makes spells and nail arts overkill. Any nail other than the Old Nail will kill a Crawlid in a single hit.
