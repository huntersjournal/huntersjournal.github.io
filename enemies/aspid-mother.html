The Aspid Mother is a flying enemy that appears in the Forgotten Crossroads, has no direct attack, and will actively avoid the player. They instead spawn Aspid Hatchlings to attack the player on their behalf.

Behavior

  • On sighting the player, an Aspid Mother will begin floating around, occasionally pausing to spawn an Aspid Hatchling while staying in mid-range distance.
  • On death, two Aspid Hatchlings will appear from the position where the Aspid Mother's body lands.

Tactics

  • While dealing with enemies which create more enemies is often a good idea, the spawned Aspid Hatchlings pose little threat, so it is rarely necessary to finish off an Aspid Mother quickly.
  • Remeber to watch out for the Aspid Hatchlings that appear once the Aspid Mother is defeated, as directly on top of where the body falls will cause them to spawn inside the player, dealing damage.
  • The Aspid Hatchlings that are spawned on death can be immediately dealt with by positioning oneself next to where the body will fall, and timing a strike to hit as they appear, killing both instantly.

Locations

Aspid Mothers can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
