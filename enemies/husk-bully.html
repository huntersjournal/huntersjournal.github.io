The Husk Bully is a ground based enemy found throught many areas of Hallownest, especially the Forgotten Crossroads and the City of Tears. It can often be found with Wandering Husks and Husk Hornheads.

Behavior

  • The Husk Bully will walk slowly from side to side, pausing every now and then, until it sights the player.
  • If the player is in its line of sight, the Husk Bully will throw itself towards the player, covering a decent distance very quickly before again pausing. Its attacks cover a shorter distance than the Wandering Husk's, but it moves much more quickly, and can attack more often.
  • The Husk Bully can lose sight of the player quite easily, in which case it will go back to wandering back and forth. If it can still see the player it will repeat its flailing attack.

Tactics

  • The Husk Bully moves too quickly during its attack to be safely batted away. Instead, it is better to remain a safe distance away, land a strike or two when they finish their attack, before backing off or otherwise evading their next attack.
  • As they are stuck on the ground, Husk Bullies can be used for nail pogoing on without much difficulty, though doing so will often trigger their barging attack.

Locations

Husk Bullies can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
