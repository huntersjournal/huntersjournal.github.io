The Winged Sentry is a flying creature that wields a nail and moves swiftly and unpredictably. It is predominately found within the City of Tears.

Behavior

  • The Winged Sentry will buzz around the player at close to medium range, before deciding to fly in for an attack.
  • For it to trigger an attack, it must be at the same height as the player. It attacks by swinging its swords once with as burst of speed in the player's direction.

Tactics

  • As the Winged Sentry can only attack when on the same level as the player, it is a good idea to keep either above or below it.
  • This can be accomplished by repeatedly nail jumping on its head, which is perhaps the strongest strategy.
  • If less comfortable with nail jumping, try to position yourself below the Winged Sentry and keeping batting it upwards.
  • While generally a bad idea to stay on its level, it can be safe to go for a hit or two right after dodgeing one of its attacks.

Locations
