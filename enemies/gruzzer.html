The Gruzzer is a simple flying enemy found exclusively in the Forgotten Crossroads. It's population severly decreases when the Forgotten Crossroads become infected.

Behavior

  • Gruzzers move diagonally, bouncing off of the walls, ceilings, and floors that they contact.
  • They move in a perfectly straight line and do not react the player's presence in any way.

Tactics

  • A Gruzzer's movement can be easily predicted, which combined with their slow speed makes them easy targets to hit.
  • Their low health makes spells and nail arts overkill. Any upgraded nail will kill a Gruzzer in a single hit.

Locations
