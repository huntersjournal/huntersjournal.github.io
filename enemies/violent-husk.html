Violent Husks appear in the Forgotten Crossroads after it has become infected, replacing many of the Wandering Husks that used the appear there. Like many infected enemies, they are much more deadly than their uninfected counterparts.

Behavior

  • The Violent Husk will charge directly towards the player on sight, not stopping until it hits either the player or a wall.
  • If the Violent Husk hits anything, or takes any damage, it will immediately explode, dealing two masks of damage to the player.

Tactics

  • Never try to use nail attacks against a Violent Husk, as this guarentees that the player will be in range of the explosion.
  • If the player has SOUL to spare, Vengeful Spirit or Shade Soul attacks can easily dispose of Violent Husks from a safe range.
  • As the Violent Husks cannot abort their charge, as long as the nearest wall is not too close, it can be simple to bait them into attacking before jumping over their charge and letting them explode far away.

Locations

Violent Husks can be found the following areas:

  • Forgotten Crossroads
