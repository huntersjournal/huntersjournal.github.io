The Husk Dandy is a small, ground enemy found within the City of Tears. Unlike the Cowardly Husk, it will face and attack the player.

Behavior

  • The Husk Dandy will take a step towards the player, then swipe eight times.
  • They will always see this attack through to completion, regardless if they player has moved elsewhere.

Tactics

  • Their range is quite short, so if you stay an appropriate distance away from them, you can hit them with your nail through their attacks.
  • It is also trival to jump over or shadow dash through them to hit them from behind.

Locations

Husk Dandies can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
