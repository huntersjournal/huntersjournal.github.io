A much larger version of the Hopper, (and with significantly more health) the Great Hopper appears across Kingdom's Edge, especially in its lower reaches.

Behavior

  • The Great Hopper will jump towards they player, aiming its leaps so that it lands where they player was a moment ago.
  • Its jumps come in quick succession, and it can cover a lot of ground rapidly.

Tactics

  • There are some locations where the player can hit the Great Hopper, but cannot get hit in turn. However, these are quite rare, and the player fill often be forced into the same area as a Great Hopper, especially in the Colosseum of Fools.
  • It is often best to approach the Great Hopper and strike, then dodge under its jump before turning and striking again. If executed properly, you should be able to walk back and forth as the Great Hopper jumps over you, hitting it as it lands.

Locations

Great Hoppers can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
