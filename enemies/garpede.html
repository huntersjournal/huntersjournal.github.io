The Journal Entry for the Garpede is unlocked by interacting with an object in Deepnest (see map for location).

The Garpede is an undamageable enemy (perhaps better thought of an an environmental obstacle) which crawls along a set path in a straight line in given intervals. Garpedes can vary wildly in speed and length.

Behavior

  • A Garpede will emerge from a wall, floor, or ceiling and travel in a straight line at a fixed speed.
  • Garpedes deal one mask of damage on contact with the player.

Tactics

  • Garpedes act as environmental hazards, and often need to be avoided with good timing, as they reappear at set time intervals.
  • They can be used for nail jumping, which is needed to complete some platforming sections.
  • The player can pass safely through a Garpede by shadow dashing.

Locations

Garpedes can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
