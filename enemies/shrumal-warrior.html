Shrumal Warriors are found in the Fungal Wastes. They fling themselves around, leaving trails of deadly gas.

Behavior

  • The Shrumal Warrior has two interchangeable attacks: a jump and a roll.
  • For the jump attack, the Shrumal Warrior curls up and flings itself in an arch, leaving a trail of gas.
  • For the roll attack, the Shrumal Warrior again curls up, before rolling along the ground at the player, leaving a trail of gas.

Tactics

  • For the jump attack, let the Shrumal Warrior sail over your head, while attacking with an Abyss Shriek or upwards strike.
  • When it rolls at you, jump over it and dash beyond the deadly gas. You can get in a downwards strike while performing this maneuver.
  • If you know it will die on the next hit, or have spaced yourself far enough away, you can face its roll head on.

Locations
