Funglings are passive floating enemies in the Fungal Wastes, similar to the Fungoon, but even less threatening. They reappear when the player exits and reenters the room, unlike most other creatures.

Behavior

  • Funglings will float gently about, taking no interest in the player.

Tactics

  • Funglings are nearly stationary, and act as good targets for nail jumping.

Locations

Funglings can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
