Sporgs are a turret-like creature found within the Fungal Wastes. Unlike most other creatures, they will reappear if you leave and reenter the room.

Behavior

  • Upon seeing the player, a Sporg will release an explosive spore which drifts towards the player. The spore explodes on contact with the player, or after a set amount of time has elapsed, dealing two masks of damage.
  • Sporgs are not limited to one explosive spore: as the first nears the end of its lifetime, it will release another.

Tactics

  • It is generally safest to ignore the Sporg, and keep moving through their areas while dodging their explosive spores.
  • If you do want to kill them, either fire spells from a distance, or lead its explosive spore away before returning for the kill.

Locations

Sporgs can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
