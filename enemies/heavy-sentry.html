The Heavy Sentry is a ground based enemy, similar in style to the Husk Sentry, though larger and slower. It too wields a nail.

Behavior

  • The Heavy Sentry has three attacks which it chooses from at random.
  • It has a charge, where the Heavy Sentry lowers its head, sticks its nail out in front, and charges in the players direction.
  • It also has a jumping attack, where it leaps up in the air and swings down with its nail as it lands.
  • Heavy Sentries can also perform this attack without jumping, though it is more rare.

Tactics

  • Keep a reasonable distance from the Heavy Sentry as it is preparing to attack, as the correct response varies. If you are too close, it is easy to get surprised by a quick downwards strike.
  • If a Heavy Sentry runs towards you (not a charge) it will execute its jumping attack next.
  • The Heavy Sentry can be hit easily as you jump over its charge attack, otherwise its best to wait for its attack to finish before counterattacking and backing off to a safe distance.

Locations

Heavy Sentries can be found the following areas:
