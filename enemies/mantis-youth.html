The Mantis Youth is a flying creature found in the Fungal Wastes near the Mantis Village. It is the younger stage of a Mantis Warrior. Unlike most other creatures, Mantis Youths will reappear if the player exits and reenters the room.

Behavior

  • When the player approaches, it drops down from its perch and begins to fly around at a medium range.
  • The Mantis Youth curls in slightly, then springs towards the player, with its sharp abdomen extended. It then recoils back towards where it began its attack.
  • Once the Mantis Lords are defeated, they will not attack unless hit first.

Tactics

  • It can be difficult to hit a Mantis Youth during their attack, as they move quickly. It is better to go after them between attacks than to try to hit them during one.
  • Dashing to avoid their attacks is quite reliable, as long as you don't dash directly towards them. If you can, try to position yourself under them when their attack finishes, so you can attack above your head when they return to their starting position.
  • If you can manage to position yourself above them, Mantis Youths cannot attack upwawrds.

Locations
