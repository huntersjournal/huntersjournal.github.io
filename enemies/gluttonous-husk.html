The Gluttonous Husk is a large, slow, jumping enemy found within the City of Tears.

Behavior

  • If no player is in sight, it will slowly walk back and forth.
  • If it sees the player, it will perform a short jump towards them. Unlike the Leaping Husk, it cannot target this attack.

Tactics

  • Gluttonous Husks make for large, easy to hit targets. You can knock them back as they jump at you, and you should be able to keep hitting them without much resistance.

Locations

Gluttonous Husks can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
