A large, passive creature found in the more open sections of Kingdom's Edge. It hovers around, taking little interest in the player.

Behavior

  • The Boofly will hover about slowly. It does not react to the player's presence in any way.

Tactics

  • If comfortable with nail jumping, that is often the quickest way to take them out, as they often get knocked out of reach when attacking upwards or sideways.

Locations

Booflys can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
