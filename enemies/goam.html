The Journal Entry for the Goam is unlocked by interacting with an object in the Forgotten Crossroads (see map for location).

The Goam is an undamageable enemy (perhaps better thought of an an environmental obstacle) which appears and disappears after a set time, and damages the player if contacted.

Behavior

  • Goams will quickly extend to full length, wait in that position for awhile, then retract just as quickly.
  • They spend equal times extended and retracted. The timing for every Goam is the same.

Tactics

  • Most Goams can be avoided with decent timing, though some groups of them require the Shade Cloak in order to pass through safely.
  • Goam worms, like other enemies, can be used for nail pogoing, even though they don't take damage. This allows the player to reach some areas earlier than otherwise.

Locations

Goams can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
