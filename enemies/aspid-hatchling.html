Aspid Hatchlings never appear alone in the world, and are instead spawned by an Aspid Mother. They are simple flying enemies that can only damage by contact and will always die in a single hit.

Behavior

  • When spawned, an Aspid Hatchling will clumsily fly towards the player, attempting to deal damage by touching them.

Tactics

  • Aspid Hatchlings can be killed in one hit, regardless of gear, and are relatively slow, making them very easy to deal with using only the nail.
  • Remeber that two Aspid Hatchlings spawn on the body of a fallen Aspid Mother: the greatest strength of Aspid Hatchlings is their small size, you may not notice them if you aren't looking out for them.
  • The Aspid Hatchlings that are spawned when an Aspid Mother dies can be immediately dealt with by positioning oneself next to where the body will fall, and timing a strike to hit as they appear, killing both instantly.

Locations

Aspid Hatchlings can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
