The Vengefly is an introductory flying enemy found in many of the starting areas, especially the Howling Cliffs and the Forgotten Crossroads. Unlike most other creatures, all Vengeflys in a room will reapear if a player simply exits the room and returns. When the Forgotten Crossroads become infected, most of the Vengeflys there are replaced by Furious Vengeflys.

Behavior

  • Vengeflys will move around slowly in random directions until they catch sight of the player.
  • Once they see the player, they will let out a squawk before more quickly flying towards the player.

Tactics

  • Vengeflys move predictably and slowly, and can be easily batted away with any normal attack.
  • Their low health makes spells and nail arts overkill. Any upgraded nail will kill a Vengefly in a single hit.

Locations

Vengeflys can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
