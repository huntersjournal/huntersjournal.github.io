The Husk Warrior is a more complex if weaker enemy found exclusively in a single room of the Fogotten Crossroads. Unlike the other husks of similar size, they wield a sword and sheild.

Behavior

  • Husk Warriors will approach the player before raising their shield, then attacking with a series of three strikes.
  • Jumping over the Husk Warrior before it begins its attack will cause it to raise its shield above its head to protect itself.
  • Attacking the Husk Warrior when it has its shield raised (either in front or above it) will deal no damage and will cause the Husk Warrior to immediately counterattack with its set of three strikes.

Tactics

  • Husk Warriors are only vulnerable to attack when they themselves are attacking, or just after their attack has finished.
  • They can be fought head on by triggering an attack, backing off, then sneaking in a hit after their attack has completed, but this is slow and has room for many mistakes.
  • Husk Warriors have no upwards attack, and so are very susceptible to nail jumping on their heads. The first downward strike will likely trigger their attack, after which the player can either continue to nail jump on them, or land on the other side and attack from behind while the Husk Warriors attack in the wrong direction.
  • Alternatively, one could attack from the front before shadow dashing through and hitting them from behind.
  • Spells are also very effective against the Husk Warriors, as their shields cannot block them. In particular, a single Shade Soul can take out both of the Husk Warriors at once.

Locations

Husk Warriors can be found the following area:
