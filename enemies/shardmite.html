A crawling enemy which, unlike the Crystal Crawler and TikTik, is stuck to only one surface, and will not transition between floor and wall, wall and ceiling, or any other combination. It protects itself by extending crystal spikes from its back.

Behavior

  • A Shardmite will crawl along until it sights the player close it it. It will then stop in place and extend its crystal spikes until they player moves further away, or out of its line of sight.
  • When its crystal spikes are extended, it is completely immune to all nail damage.

Tactics

  • Shardmite can be damaged with a nail by quickly jumping or falling towards it and hitting it before it can fully extend its spikes.
  • They are also always vulnerable to spell damage, as well as effects such as Defender's Crest.

Locations

Shardmites can be found the following areas:

  • Howling Cliffs
  • Forgotten Crossroads
  • Greenpath
  • Resting Grounds
