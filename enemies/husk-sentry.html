The Husk Sentry is a nimble ground based creature found within the City of Tears. It is one of the few creatures whose health depends on the players current nail upgrade.

Behavior

  • The Husk Sentry will unpredictably execute one of its three moves: a leap back, a single strike, or a triple strike.
  • For its leap back, the Husk Sentry which quickly step backwards away from the player, putting a large amount of distance between them.
  • If the Husk Sentry is in range, it may either swipe once at them player, or swing three times while moving forwards.

Tactics

  • Husk Sentries can be unpredictable, as their single and triple strikes start the same. In general, it is safer to assume that they will execute a triple strike, even though the rarer of the two moves.
  • They have no upwards attack, so it is often safe to nail jump on their heads.
  • Otherwise, you can quickly come in, hit them once, then either retreat immediately, jump over, or shadow dash through their attack.

Locations

Husk Sentrys can be found the following areas:
